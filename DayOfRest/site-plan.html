Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website to provide recommendations for movies on sunday. Some people have varying views on what movies should and shouldn't be viewed on sunday. We hope with with our recommendations that people will be able to find movies to watch on sunday.

Audience

My target audience will be mostly be christians or those wanting to watch a wholesome movie on the sabbath day. Also it could target family trying to enjoy a good sunday movie.

Dynamic elements

I plan to create javascript entries for movie recommendations. I will also make some functions to filter the movies by a ranking system. Also will research a way to save user input for there rankings on the movies.
